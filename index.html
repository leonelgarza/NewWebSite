About me

My name is Leonel Garza Loperena, I was born in Ciudad Victoria, Tamaulipas in Mexico on July 8th, 1986, I'm married and have a daughter, currently live in Apodaca, Nuevo Leon in Mexico and I like to learn new things like math, history and geography, I also love to discover new places and travel. I like to watch sports like football, soccer, and formula 1. I am currently learning to program in new programming languages.


I studied Bachelor in Computer Science at the Faculty of Physical and Mathematical Sciences and a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering at the Faculty of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering both at the Autonomous University of Nuevo León. I have worked for the last 12 years at Prolec where I started as a Software Development Engineer, programming the design rules for transformers electrical distribution, and from 2019 to date I work as a Development Engineer at Prolec Labs in projects of development and innovation of new products and applications.
